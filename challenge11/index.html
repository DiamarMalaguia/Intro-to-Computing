Japan, officially known as the "Land of the Rising Sun," is an East Asian island nation located in the Pacific Ocean. Here's a brief description of Japan:

Geography

Japan consists of four main islands—Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku—along with numerous smaller islands. It is known for its diverse geography, including mountainous terrain, dense forests, and a myriad of hot springs. Mount Fuji, an iconic symbol of Japan, is the country's highest peak.
